How It Works

From open shift to confirmed nurse in three steps.

ShiftBridge handles the handoff between facilities, agencies, and nurses — automatically, at every step.

1

Post a Shift

Facilities post open shifts by credential type — RN, LPN, CNA, CMA. Agencies can post on behalf of facilities they haven't yet connected. Shifts appear on the calendar the moment they're saved.

[ Screenshot: Shift Post Form ] Drop your screenshot here
2

Nurses Claim

Qualified nurses see open shifts matching their credential — with drive time estimates from their home address. One tap to claim. Built-in conflict detection prevents double-booking before it happens.

[ Screenshot: Nurse Shift Calendar ] Drop your screenshot here
3

Confirm & Done

Facility admins review full credential cards for every claimant and confirm with one click. The winning nurse gets an SMS. All other claimants are notified automatically. Zero phone calls required.

Platform Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Built specifically for per diem nursing staffing — not adapted from generic workforce software that wasn't designed for healthcare.

Real-Time Shift Calendars

Live month-view calendars for every role. Post a shift and it appears instantly across all dashboards. Status changes propagate in real time — no refresh needed.

Credential Management

License number, status, and expiry. CPR, TB test date, and COVID vaccination — all tracked per nurse with automatic 30-day expiry warnings. Facilities never confirm a nurse blind.

Drive Time Estimates

Nurses see "~18 min away" on every shift card before they commit. Calculated from their registered home address via Google Maps — no extra setup required.

Smart Notifications

In-app, SMS, and email — automatically triggered at every step. Claim received, shift confirmed, shift canceled, DNR issued, credential expiring. Nothing slips through.

Agency↔Facility Connections

Agencies build a roster of connected facilities. Smart address matching detects existing relationships automatically. Placeholder facilities bridge the gap for partners not yet on the platform.

CSV Export & Billing

One-click export of all shift data by month — date, facility, credential, hours worked, tier, pay rate, nurse license number, and late-cancel flags. Payroll-ready, every time.
